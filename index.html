Our Space

World-Class Facilities

Every corner of Loving Homes has been designed with canine comfort and enrichment at its heart.

What We Offer
01 Grooming Parlour & Spa
+
Our fully-equipped grooming parlour offers everything from a simple wash & brush to a full spa treatment. Experienced groomers handle all breeds with patience and expertise, leaving your dog looking and feeling their absolute best.
02 24-Hour Veterinary Services
+
A qualified vet is on-site around the clock. Whether it's a routine check or an unexpected health concern, your dog is in safe hands at all times. We'll always keep you informed and seek your consent for any treatment.
03 Luxury Heated Suites
+
Each private suite features underfloor heating or cooling depending on the season, a plush orthopaedic bed, and in-room TV entertainment. Dogs sleep soundly in their own space, free from unnecessary stress.
04 Woodland Walks & Paddock
+
Our property borders a private woodland trail used for scenic, off-lead exploration. We also have a fully secure outdoor paddock for free-roaming play and an indoor activity barn for rainy days.
05 Chauffeur Collect & Return
+
Door-to-door chauffeur service available within a 25-mile radius. Your dog travels in a climate-controlled, luxury vehicle with a familiar handler — making drop-off and pick-up completely stress-free for both of you.
06 Pet-Watch Monitoring
+
Our Pet-Watch system gives you peace of mind with live camera access to your dog's suite and activity areas via our app. Receive real-time notifications, photos, and short video clips throughout their stay.
